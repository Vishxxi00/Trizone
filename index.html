ABOUT US

TRIZONE

We mix passion and purpose at TRIZONE to develop and produce smart devices, systems, software, and service that link people no matter where they are. To inspire breakthrough performances that produce customer value and high shareholder returns, we lead with integrity, innovate with purpose, and drive operational excellence. We began as a modest business with a capital of only 190,000 dollars and a staff of about 20 people. J D Thomas, the company's founder, stated that the goal was to "create an ideal factory that emphasizes a culture of freedom and open mindedness that will, via technology, transform the world." Trizone creates products that improve people's lives by simplifying how they engage with technology and connecting them to the world around them. By enabling rich experiences through the connected automobile, connected enterprise, and connected lifestyle, our strong workforce and innovation strength produce value for our stakeholders. Trizone is a global online marketplace with ecommerce stores in Sri Lanka, UK, China, France and Germany.
